SUBSCRIBE TO CMCS

How do I subscribe?

If you would like to subscribe to the journal, please print the order form, which has information on payment methods. Alternatively, e-mail us at cmcspub@gmail.com and we will be happy to sort out any questions you have.

How much does it cost?

Subscription costs £10 per annum (£30 for libraries).

What do I get?

You will receive two volumes of Cambrian Medieval Celtic Studies each year: a Summer and a Winter issue. A renewal form will be sent out with the Winter volume.
