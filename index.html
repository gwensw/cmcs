ABOUT US

CMCS Publications is a small academic company specialising in works related to the discipline of Celtic Studies. We publish Cambrian Medieval Celtic Studies (CMCS), a bi-annual, peer-reviewed academic journal. The cost of a yearly subscription for individuals — £10 — has remained unchanged since the journal's founding in 1981. The editor is Patrick Sims-Williams.

In addition to the journal, we also publish a range of books on Celtic topics.

If you are interested in submitting an article to the CMCS journal, or if you have any queries, please contact cmcspub@gmail.com
